What is it?

In a sea of things that need annotation, you would be lucky to find a Wordfish. We are developing an annotation tool for experts to label things. It is an effort toward collaborative annotation to produce publicly available datasets for research. Wordfish will (eventually) allow for a combination of human- and algorithm- derived annotations of reports. This work is being done by the Langlotzlab at Stanford University.
